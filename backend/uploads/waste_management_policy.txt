SMART CITY MUNICIPAL AUTHORITY
OFFICIAL POLICY DOCUMENT: RESIDENTIAL WASTE MANAGEMENT & RECYCLING
Document Reference: SC-WM-2024-V2
Section 1: General Guidelines
Residents must place their waste and recycling bins at the curbside no earlier than 6:00 PM on the evening before their scheduled collection day, and no later than 7:00 AM on the morning of collection. Bins must be retrieved from the curbside and returned to the side or rear of the property by 7:00 PM on the day of collection. 

Section 2: Late Bin Placement Fines
If a resident fails to retrieve their bin from the curbside by the 7:00 PM deadline, they will receive a written warning for the first offense. For a second offense within a calendar year, a fine of $25 will be issued. For a third or subsequent offense, the fine is increased to $75. Late bin placements impede street cleaning services and create sidewalk obstructions.

Section 3: Sorting Regulations
Bins are color-coded for sorting accuracy:
- Blue Bins: Recyclable clean paper, cardboard, clean aluminum cans, and plastic bottles (PET 1 and HDPE 2). Plastic bags and polystyrene foam are strictly prohibited in blue bins.
- Green Bins: Organic waste, food scraps, yard clippings, and compostable paper products.
- Black Bins: General non-recyclable residential household waste.

Section 4: Hazardous Waste Disposal
Batteries, electronics, mercury thermometers, fluorescent bulbs, paints, and oils must not be disposed of in standard residential bins. Residents must take these materials to the Smart City Environmental Center located at 450 E. Clean Water Avenue. Disposal of hazardous materials in residential bins is subject to a civil penalty fine of up to $500.

Section 5: Scheduled Collection Days by District
- District 1: Monday (General Waste), Thursday (Recycling & Organics)
- District 2: Tuesday (General Waste), Friday (Recycling & Organics)
- District 3: Wednesday (General Waste), Saturday (Recycling & Organics)
- District 4: Thursday (General Waste), Tuesday (Recycling & Organics)
- District 5: Friday (General Waste), Wednesday (Recycling & Organics)
- District 6: Saturday (General Waste), Monday (Recycling & Organics)
