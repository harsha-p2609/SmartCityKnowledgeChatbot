SMART CITY TRANSIT & MOBILITY COMMISSION
OFFICIAL CODE: URBAN TRAFFIC AND VEHICULAR REGULATIONS
Document Reference: SC-TR-2025-V1
Section 1: Speed Limits by Zone
To ensure citizen safety and reduce traffic accidents, speed limits are strictly enforced across all city sectors:
- Residential Zones: The maximum speed limit is 25 miles per hour (mph).
- School and Playground Zones: The speed limit is 15 mph when children are present or when yellow lights are flashing.
- Commercial Downtown Districts: The speed limit is 30 mph.
- Urban Expressways: The speed limit is 55 mph unless posted otherwise.

Section 2: Speeding Penalties
Fines for speeding in Smart City are calculated on a tiered basis:
- 1 to 10 mph over the limit: A fine of $50 is issued.
- 11 to 20 mph over the limit: A fine of $150 is issued, along with 2 points on the driver's license.
- More than 20 mph over the limit: A fine of $300 is issued, along with a mandatory court appearance and 4 points on the license.
- All speeding fines are doubled if committed within a school zone or active road construction zone.

Section 3: Smart Parking Regulations
All municipal parking spots are metered via the Smart City Mobis app. Parking rates are $1.50 per hour in residential clusters and $3.00 per hour in commercial zones.
- Fire Hydrants: Parking within 15 feet of a fire hydrant is strictly illegal. Vehicles parked within 15 feet of a hydrant will be towed immediately at the owner's expense and fined $100.
- Disabled Parking Spaces: Unauthorized parking in spaces marked for disabled individuals is subject to a $250 fine.
- Tow-Away Zones: Parking is prohibited on major expressways and designated transit corridors between 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM - 6:30 PM (peak commuter hours). Violators will be towed.
